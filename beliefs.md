### Beliefs

The world can always be made a better place, 
but that only happens when individuals take responsibility for their actions.
Each and every decision a person makes is important, large or small.
The choices we make individually snowball into the reality we collectively experience day to day.

A few good actions to take:

- Exercise every day to improve health and happiness
- Buy less stuff; keep only what you need and what makes you very happy
- Eat things that aren't animals to reduce carbon footprint and to cause less pain
- Interact face to face with as many people as often as possible
